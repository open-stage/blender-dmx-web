## Improved MVR Classing and Layers management

Explicit management of MVR Layers has been added. MVR Layers management could
historically be done in an implicit way through Blender collections, new Layers
management adds easy add/rename/remove/hide/show/select/assign/unassign
options. Same management has also been added to MVR Classes.

* Initial MVR Class management
* Initial explicit MVR Layer management
* Allow to export active layer only
