## Full Scene MVR Export Added

Both pymvr and pygdtf libraries have been much worked on in the past several
weeks, resulting in the possibility to finally implement an initial Full Scene
MVR Export to BlenderDMX (previously, only fixtures had been exported).

**Full Changelog**: https://github.com/open-stage/blender-dmx/compare/v2.0.19...v2.1.0
