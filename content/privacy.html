Website

When you visit our website, we don't collect any data and we don't store any cookies.

Website statistics are utilizing Goat Counter - a privacy friendly web counter without any tracking of personal data.

BlenderDMX

BlenderDMX runs locally on your computer inside Blender. At no time will any of your stored personal data be sent to us or a third party unless you initialize the action for example via MVR-xchange.

BlenderDMX can log debug information to a file. You can set the level of debug detail included. If you send the logfile to someone for debugging, check that it does not contain private, sensitive, or not-disclosable data first.
